Homemade Tandoori Chicken Kati Rolls Recipe



This recipe for tandoori chicken kati rolls will be a surefire hit whether you are
entertaining for a crowd or trying to feed young picky eaters!


Tender tandoori chicken packs major flavor with the addition of carmalized red onions, wrapped up in a flaky paratha, topped with homemade white sauce to cool the palate, and cucumber to add a delightful crunch.
Trust me when I say these Kati Rolls satisfy one and all!
